MusicShop - checklist za flashka

Preporachitelen variant:
- Kopirai cqlata papka `musicshop` bez preimenuvane.

Zaduljitelni runtime elementi:
- vseki osnoven `.html` fail v korena
- papka `api/`
- papka `css/`
- papka `js/`
- papka `images/`
- fail `musicshop_last.sql`

Ne preimenuvai papkata:
- proektut tryabva da stoi kato `musicshop`
- v koda ima zahardkodeni putishta `/musicshop/api/...`

Nezaduljitelni:
- `documentation/`
- `musicshop.sql`
- `seed_imaginary_news_20260325.sql`
- `product_description_updates_20260326.sql`
- `start_stack.py`
- `stop_stack.py`
- `reset_db_kala.sh`

Ako iskash instalaciq bez internet:
- dobavi i instalator za XAMPP na flashkata
