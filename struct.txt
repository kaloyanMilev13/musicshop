04.12 -  w momenta wersiqta na index e gore-dolu stabilna
--------------------------------------------------------------

05.12 - izkluchitelnoo mnogo promeni. dizainut weche se priblijawa kum finalen stage. ima blueprint page-owe za producti. ima snimki za tqh. ostawat oshte neshta kato: uprawlenie na nowinite, adminski panel, overall cwetowe

• - Dark theme readability improved further: headings, product prices, hero titles, and
    brand titles now switch to light colors when dark mode is active.
  - Product browsing flows now go to a dedicated detail page instead of the modal:
      - products.html “View details” links to product.html?id=....
      - favorites.html “View details” also links to the same.
      - Cart items include a “View product” button that opens product.html?id=<id>.
  - Added a redesigned product detail blueprint at product.html: two-column layout (image
    + details), large price, meta tags, and add-to-cart/favorite actions. It’s driven
    by api/products.php?id=... and uses the site’s shared styling. Responsive on smaller
    screens.
  - Kept modal code only where still needed; products/favorites no longer trigger it.

  Now every product has a single detail page (product.html?id=123), and text should remain
  readable in dark mode.
